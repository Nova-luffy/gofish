📜 Official Rules

  • Choose an opponent and ask them for a specific card rank (2 through Ace).
  • If the opponent has cards of that rank, they must give them to you.
  • If they do not have that rank, they will tell you to "Go Fish!" and you must draw a card from the deck.
  • When you collect all 4 suits of a single rank, it forms a Fold. The player with the most folds wins!
